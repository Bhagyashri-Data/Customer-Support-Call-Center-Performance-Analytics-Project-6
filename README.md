# Customer-Support-Call-Center-Performance-Analytics-Project-6

# 📊 Project Conclusion: Call Center Performance Analysis

This end-to-end data analytics project successfully integrates **SQL, Python (Pandas), Advanced Excel, and Power BI** to analyze and visualize raw call center logs. By transforming messy data into an interactive business dashboard, we uncovered key operational insights.

---

### 🔍 1. Business Management & Agent Performance
* **Anjali:** Handled the highest volume with **4 successful calls**, demonstrating strong efficiency and workload management.
* **Rahul & Rohan:** Balanced the remaining workload, handling **3 calls each**.
* **Total Volume:** A total of **10 customer interactions** were processed and analyzed during this evaluation period.

---

### 📉 2. Customer Satisfaction (CSAT) Metrics
* **Satisfied Customers:** **60% of customers** (6 out of 10) reported a positive experience, giving a CSAT rating of 4 or 5.
* **Dissatisfied Customers:** **40% of customers** (4 out of 10) expressed dissatisfaction, giving a CSAT rating below 4. This highlights a critical area for service quality improvement.

---

### 🛠️ 3. Technical Milestones & Core Learnings
1. **Data Manipulation (SQL):** Applied advanced techniques like `CASE WHEN`, `GROUP BY`, and aggregate functions (`COUNT`) to categorize and segment raw data directly from the database.
2. **Data Export (Python Pandas):** Leveraged `to_csv(index=False)` to export clean data structures without cluttering the final dataset with unnecessary row index columns.
3. **Data Summarization (Advanced Excel):** Utilized Pivot Tables and column auto-fitting to restructure data into corporate-ready summary tables.
4. **Interactive Dashboarding (Power BI):** Developed a dynamic dashboard using a Donut Chart and an interactive Slicer tool, allowing stakeholders to filter performance metrics by specific call topics instantly.

---

**💡 Final Business Recommendation:** With a 40% dissatisfaction rate, the management should focus on targeted agent training for unresolved 'Pending' issues, specifically focusing on complex customer complaints like 'Billing Issues' and 'Technical Support'.

Tools Used: SQL (Data Extraction), Python Pandas (Data Handling), Advanced Excel (Pivot & Basic Charting), Power BI (Interactive Slicers & Donut Dashboard).
